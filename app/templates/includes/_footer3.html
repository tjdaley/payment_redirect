Attorney.Bot CRM

Attorney.Bot CRM is a client relationship management portal that tracks clients from prospect to completed engagement. Attorney.Bot CRM gathers all essential information about a client relationship into a single portal and provides tools to standardize and streamline attorney work product and communications.
